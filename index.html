Who I Am

I gave my business the name Tiny Panther in honor of my own personal tiny panther, Preeti. I feel very strongly about changing the stigma surrounding black cats. They are very often the last ones to be adopted in a shelter, if at all. And they are also most likely to suffer harm from those who are misinformed. I hope that through efforts like mine, people will understand that ALL cats deserve your love and care, regardless of the color of their fur.
